Good Data = Good Campaigns

At Data Farm, we believe effective outreach starts with reliable data. For over two decades, we have supported progressive campaigns, PACs, and advocacy organizations with the data infrastructure they need to compete and win.

From national political committees to local elections, our work has powered fundraising programs, voter contact efforts, issue advocacy campaigns, and grassroots mobilization initiatives. Across every level of the ballot, one principle remains constant: campaigns succeed when their data is accurate, organized, and actionable.

Services

Mobile Phone Appends

Phone and text programs need mobile phones. We can validate your phones to make your existing list more effective and we can add mobile phones to your lists so you can reach more people. We use multiple sources when appending phones to get the highest possible quality phones added back to your list.

Email Appends

Need to grow your email list? We can add emails to your existing mail or phone lists so you can expand the ways you reach your supporters.

Organized Data

We know your fundraising lists come from many sources and are a combination of old and new data. We can standardize, merge and prioritize your lists into one clean, easily usable list. Campaigns work with many platforms with specialized formatting needs. We customize your data to your needs so your team can get up and running with new data quickly
